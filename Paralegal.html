SERVICES OF A PARALEGAL

Paralegals are professionals who, by education, training or experience, gain the knowledge and skills to engage in law-related work. In many countries like the US, UK, they play an important supportive role to lawyers, law firms, corporations, governmental and other agencies.

However, in India there is no such recognised role. The Paralegal (PL) assists with case planning, development, and management, legal research, interviews clients, gathers facts, retrieves information, analyzes and collects legal documents like Encumberance Certificates, tax-records etc... to make recommendations to an Attorney.
There are different kinds of paralegals: (i) Real estate Paralegal (ii) Litigation Paralegal (iii) Corporate Paralegal (iv) Freelance Paralegal etc...The need for Paralegals is directly related to the demand for lawyers in the country. India definitely needs a lot of lawyers, hence, the demand for paralegals are also on the increase.

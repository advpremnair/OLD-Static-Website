PARALEGAL - SERVICES

Paralegals (PL) are professionals who, by education, training or experience, gain the knowledge and skills to engage in law-related work. In many developed countries, like the US, UK, they play an important supportive role to lawyers, law firms, corporations, governmental and other agencies.

However, in India till recently there is no such recognised PL-role. But with the launching of the Tele-Law – Reaching the Unreached – Programme, the first generation Paralegals are officially recognized by the Department of justice, under the Central Ministry of Law.

The Paralegals were entrusted with the duty of carrying-out initial paper works and organising the meetings of the beneficiaries with the Panel Lawyers and to do the necessary follow-up works in the Tele-Law Programme.

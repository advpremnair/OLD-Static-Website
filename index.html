The Paralegal's services are comparatively new in India, recently the Ministry of Law and Justice, offered first generation Paralegal's services, under its Tele-Law Project - using technology for providing legal-advice to the Citizens hitherto unreachable.

Originally paralegal's work include researching, collecting and investigating Legal documents, like title-encumbrance certificate, land tax receipts, information about tax-liabilities etc.

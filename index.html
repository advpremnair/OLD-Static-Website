Paralegal services

The Paralegal's services are comparatively new in India, recently the Ministry of Law and Justice, offered Paralegal's services, under its Tele-Law Project.

Paralegal's work include researching, collecting and investigating Legal documents, like title-encumbrance certificate, information about tax-liabilities etc.

There are several kinds of paralegals, for eg: Real estate paralegal, Litigation paralegal, etc.. We offer Real estate Paralegal services. To know more click on ‘Paralegal’ Page or 'Read more'.
